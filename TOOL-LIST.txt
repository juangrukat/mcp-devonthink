Unsupported operations I found:
- delete — Buggy AppleScript generation (doubles "record" keyword)
- move — Buggy AppleScript generation
- Database-level operations (close, compress) — Limited by UUID/object handling

The link tools (linkauditrecord, linkmapneighborhood, linksuggestrelated, linkfindorphans, linkmaintenancepass, linkrepairlinks, etc.) are quite powerful for graph analysis of your knowledge base.
